Vegeterian Ramen

Description

Rustle up this warming veggie ramen in just 15 minutes. Think of it as healthy fast food – it's low-fat, low-calorie and low-cost as well

Ingredients

  • 80g instant noodles
  • 2 spring onions, finely chopped
  • ½ head pak choi
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds
  • chili sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles with the sachet of flavouring provided. Add the spring onions and pak choi for the final min.
  2. Meanwhile, simmer the egg for 6 mins from boiling, run it under cold water to stop it cooking, then peel it. Toast the sesame seeds in a frying pan.
  3. Tip the noodles and greens into a deep bowl, halve the boiled egg and place on top. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, then drizzle with the sauce or sesame oil provided with the noodles, and chilli sauce, if using.
